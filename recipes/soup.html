Mushroom Soup

Description

This dish is made with a mixture of the most degedant Mushroom in a boiling pot of flavour. Hearty, filling and delickious

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms
  • Butter
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Cut and remove mushroom stems
  2. Heat up butter and add onion. Caramalise the onions
  3. Add mushrooms and cook down
  4. Add cream
  5. Blend and serve
